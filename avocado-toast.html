Time: 10 min • Servings: 2

A crunchy, creamy classic. Add eggs, tomato, or chili flakes to level it up.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: chili flakes, fried egg, cherry tomatoes

Steps

  1. Toast bread to your liking.
  2. Mash avocado with lemon, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread on toast and add optional toppings.
