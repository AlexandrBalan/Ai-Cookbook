Time: 5 min • Servings: 2

Cold, fruity, and ready in minutes. Great for breakfast or post-workout.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 banana
  • ¾ cup yogurt (or dairy-free)
  • ½ cup milk (or dairy-free)
  • Honey or maple syrup to taste

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth; add liquid if too thick.
  3. Taste and sweeten as needed.
