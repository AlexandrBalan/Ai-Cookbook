Time: 30–35 min • Servings: 4

Creamy, comforting pasta with juicy chicken and a simple Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt, pepper, garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water; reserve ½ cup pasta water.
  2. Sauté chicken with salt/pepper until cooked.
  3. Melt butter, add cream; simmer 2–3 min.
  4. Whisk in Parmesan until smooth; adjust with pasta water.
  5. Toss pasta and chicken in sauce; serve warm.
